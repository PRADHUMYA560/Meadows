PLANTING CAMPAIGN IN SCHOOL

Generally on Birthdays, students distribute candies among their schoolmate, the wrappers of these candies are made of palstic and its use is contributing in Pollution. Hence, our Respected Principal Sir, Dr. D.K. Sinha started a campaign. According to it on Birthdays no candies would be distributed but instead student's will plant a sapling in school premises and many student's followed it responsibly. Not only this our Principal gift a potted spaling to every Special and Chief Guests that are invited to different events organised in our school...
